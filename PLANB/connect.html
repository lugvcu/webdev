Connect with the Linux User Group of VCU

Email Disscussion List

The discussion list is an email list for those who want to receive all the business email and minor notices. There may quite a few posts to this list per week. If you want to only receive priority email, just join the regular list (above). You can join the discussion list. If you join this list, you may post general questions or comments to the club here.

Email Announcement List

Please feel free to join the LUGVCU announcements list. You must use your VCU email address to be approved without question. This email list receives only a couple posts per week on average. You can also unsubscribe at the same link above. Another way to subscribe or unsubscribe is to send an email to "VCU-LUG-[un]subscribe-request@lists.vcu.edu". (Remove the "[un]" to subscribe...)
